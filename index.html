Introduction

Ever wanted to have a go at animation? I found an old copy of Anime Debut 6 on the internet for a few pounds and decided I would take a look. It was mind boggling at first as I had never animated anything, ever! I had no idea what I was looking at or how to use it.

Not wanting to be put off I turned to the endless oracle of tutorials...Youtube! In no time I had spliced together a very short animation featuring my Mum and a very annoying bird. Was it good? No, no it was not. None the less I really wanted to have a go at creating something a bit better.

I ended up making a short film for a freind of mine with whome I share a mutual love of gaming. it took many hours of learning, many hours of figuring out why things were not working the way they should and a long time after being very proud of what I had made.

my goal here is to create a space that anyone who is interested in having a go at basic animation can use to find a place to start. Here you will find a range of resourses available to use at will, software recomendations and a growing community of people interested in supporting and guiding each other.

It really doesnt matter if you are any good at art or not, animation is so much more than that. You can tell a story with stick men after all!
